Tyresse okatch's recepies

description

a simple and tasty home made recepie prepared by tyresse okatch.enjoy

ingridients

  • maize flour 2 cups
  • 3 cups water
  • red meat 500g
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 tablespoon tomatoe paste
  • beef broth
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • fresh coriander
  • salt
  • fresh basil

instructions

  1. boil the beef with 1 cup of water
  2. saute the onions and garlic and add the mincedgarlic and ginger
  3. and the tomatoes and spices.
  4. pour the beef broth then let it simmer until the stew thickens.
  5. garnish with fresh coriander
  6. in a large sufuria boil 3 cups of water
  7. add maize flour gradually
  8. mix and cook
  9. reduce the heat and let it cook for 3-5 minutes while turning occasionally
  10. shape the ugali and serve with the beef stew while hot
